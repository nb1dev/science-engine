Understanding your report

Your gut is at the
centre of everything

Most people think of the gut as a digestive organ. In reality it's a master communicator — constantly receiving signals from your lifestyle and sending signals back out to your immune system, brain, hormones, heart, and more. Your microbiome report maps this ecosystem.
